# How to Use Dark Mode - User Guide

## Enabling Dark Mode

### Step 1: Open Settings
1. Open the app
2. Tap the menu icon (â˜°) in the top left
3. Select **Settings** from the drawer

### Step 2: Toggle Dark Mode
1. Scroll to the **Preferences** section
2. Find **Dark Mode** option
3. Tap the toggle switch to turn it ON
4. The app will instantly switch to dark mode!

### Step 3: Enjoy!
- Your preference is automatically saved
- Dark mode will remain active even after closing the app
- All screens will use the dark theme

## Disabling Dark Mode

1. Go back to **Settings**
2. Find **Dark Mode** under Preferences
3. Tap the toggle switch to turn it OFF
4. The app will instantly switch back to light mode

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Will my data be affected?
**A:** No, dark mode only changes colors. Your data remains unchanged.

### Q: Does it work offline?
**A:** Yes, dark mode works whether you're online or offline.

### Q: Can I schedule it?
**A:** Not yet, but this feature may be added in future updates.

### Q: Does it drain more battery?
